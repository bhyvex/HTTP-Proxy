Why not use Squid?

  • Let me make this clear for you all: Squid is meant for CACHING.

  • Mmm, high-performance caching.

  • HTTP::Proxy gives you total control over your web experience.

  • If you want speed, cache and some kind of filtering, you can leave the room and continue to use Squid.

  • If you want TOTAL CONTROL of the web, you can stay a little longer. ;-)
